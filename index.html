🏈 How to Play Football Bros – Controls & Winning Strategies

🎮 Movement: WASD or Arrow keys to move your player around the field.
🏃 Sprint / Stiff Arm: Hold SHIFT to sprint — bump into defenders to break tackles.
🤾 Pass / Switch player: Press SPACE or X to throw the ball to an open teammate (when on offense). On defense, SPACE switches to the nearest defender.
💥 Tackle / Dive: While on defense, press S or Down Arrow to dive-tackle the ball carrier.
🏆 Special Move: Press Q or E to perform a “Bro Power” (stumble, juke, or heavy hit when your meter is full).
🎯 Goal: Reach the end zone to score a touchdown (6 points) then go for an extra point or 2-point conversion. The team with most points after 2-minute halves wins!
🧠 Pro tip for Football Bros: Use stiff-arm (Shift) right before a tackle to push defenders away. On defense, anticipate the runner's direction and dive sideways. Also, master the lateral pass: you can toss the ball backwards to keep the play alive — pure chaos!

Advanced strategy: In Football Bros, don't just run straight — zigzag to avoid clusters. When playing multiplayer, coordinate passes and set up “bro blocks” to clear the path. Use the dive tackle only when close; miss and you'll leave a huge gap. The game rewards aggressive yet clever plays, so be unpredictable.
