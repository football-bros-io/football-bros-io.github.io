How to Play Football Bros

  1. Game Setup: Choose your team and customize your players before starting a match.
  2. Controls: Use the arrow keys to move your player. The 'A' key passes the ball, while the 'S' key shoots. Press 'D' to tackle opponents when defending.
  3. Offense: Maintain possession by passing between players. Look for openings in the defense to take shots on goal.
  4. Defense: Position your players to intercept passes. Time your tackles carefully to avoid fouls.
  5. Special Moves: Build up your special meter by completing passes and making tackles. Once full, activate powerful special moves with the 'Space' key.
  6. Winning: Score more goals than your opponent before time runs out to win the match!

With multiple game modes including tournament, quick match, and penalty shootouts, Football Bros offers variety and excitement for all types of football fans.
